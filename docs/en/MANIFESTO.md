## Three Declarations

### 1. We end the lie that "decentralized is slow."

Decentralized services have been slow, heavy, and hard. So nobody used them.

MORM is different.

Three-second WebM segments (**MORM Cells**) and a 50% / 10% predictive caching cycle. The moment a viewer reaches 1.5 seconds, the old data is purged. The moment they reach 0.3 seconds, the next cell is reserved. Devices stay light. Playback never stalls.

We will deliver a faster experience than TikTok — without servers.
That is the first thing MORM proves.

### 2. We take back an internet without censorship.

We end the era when expression vanishes overnight because of a corporate policy change.

MORM is built on a P2P gateway with no IP restrictions, on top of a fully distributed blockchain (**MORM Chain**). There is no central switch. No one can pull the plug.

But this is not lawlessness.
Copies and unauthorized clips are rejected at upload by V-Hash and audio fingerprinting. AI-generated works are anchored to the chain by a **Generation ID**, protecting the very first instance. Only originals remain.

Freedom and purity coexist.

### 3. We build an economy where lying is impossible.

MORM Shop will be the hardest place in the world to commit fraud.

Senders record packing videos. Buyers record unboxing videos. Both are captured by a dedicated camera, with the latest block hash burned into the video as a watermark. Tampering becomes mathematically infeasible.

When fraud is detected, smart contracts automatically refund the buyer. The fraudster's node is frozen (**Node-Lock**), their stake is confiscated (**Slash**), and they are permanently exiled from the network at the biometric-identity level.

No middlemen. No fake reviews.
Only physical evidence that cannot be tampered with.

## Our Promises

- **Operator fee is 1%.** Locked into a smart contract, never to be changed. The remaining 99% is returned to users and nodes.
- **You don't have to remember a wallet.** No seed phrases, no private keys. Your device's biometric authentication is the signature.
- **Data belongs to those who create it.** Creator rights are guarded by Generation ID and V-Hash.
- **We will remain a Swarm.** We have no center. We will never build a system where one person can shut it down.
- **We have no legal entity.** No Corporation. No Foundation. No DAO LLC. Only Smart Contracts, AI Agents, and the DAO operate this network. Nothing stops because of any one party's interests.
