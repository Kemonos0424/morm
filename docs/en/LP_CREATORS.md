## FAQ (for Creators)

### Q. How do I migrate my existing followers?

A. MORM provides a "Cross-Platform Migrate" tool. Just paste your MORM profile link in your existing SNS bio — your MORM account is verified as "the real you."

### Q. What if I don't want to disclose AI generation?

A. Recording a Generation ID is **optional**. It's only anchored when you explicitly mark it. Externally generated AI videos can also use C2PA manifests for provenance protection.

### Q. Who owns my videos?

A. **You do, fully.** MORM only takes the licensing necessary for network operation (storage, delivery, transcoding).

### Q. Will I get suspended for terms violations?

A. Censorship is impossible, but universally illegal content (CSAM, terrorism, trafficking, etc.) is removed via AI screening + community reports + Validator review. Political opinions, religious views, or sexual expression will not result in bans.

### Q. Can I use MORM alongside existing platforms?

A. Of course. MORM is designed to function as a "safety net." Use multiple platforms and treat MORM as your fallback.

### Q. How do I withdraw earnings?

A. Swap to other crypto inside the network and withdraw. Or bridge via wMORM (post-Swarm) to Ethereum/Solana and convert to fiat via local exchanges.
