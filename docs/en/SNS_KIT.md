## 7. Visual Guidelines

- Background: Void Black (#000) or deep dark gray
- Accents: electric blue / violet / amber
- Always include at least one swarm-particle motif
- Logo bottom-right or center
- Fonts: geometric sans-serif (Inter / Geist / Noto Sans)

---

## 8. Don'ts

- "The next TikTok killer" — frame around our own world, not comparisons
- Unfounded superlatives like "the best Web3 ever," "fastest in the industry"
- Yield/return marketing for investors (securities-law risk)
- Definitive launch dates (use Phases instead)
- Direct attacks on other Web3 projects
