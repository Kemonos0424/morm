## Proof of Physical Evidence (PoPE)

The heart of MORM Shop. **First commercial implementation in the world.**

### How it works

```
[Seller]                       [Buyer]
   │                             │
   ├─ Record packing video       │
   │  via dedicated camera       │
   │  (with block-hash watermark)│
   ├─ Anchor video hash on-chain │
   ├─ Ship                       │
   │                             │
   │                             ├─ Receive
   │                             ├─ Record unboxing video
   │                             │  via dedicated camera
   │                             │  (with block-hash watermark)
   │                             └─ Anchor video hash on-chain
   ↓                             ↓
   ┌──────────────────────────────┐
   │ AI / Validator integrity check │
   │ ・Motion analysis              │
   │ ・Content matching             │
   │ ・Timestamp consistency        │
   └──────────────────────────────┘
            ↓                ↓
      [Match] release       [Mismatch] auto-refund + Node-Lock
```

### Why fraud is impossible

1. **Block-hash watermark** — videos burn the chain state at capture time into every frame. No re-shoot can match the hash.
2. **Dedicated camera required** — the capture app rejects fakes. AI-generated videos are detected.
3. **Motion-analysis AI** — unnatural cuts, loops, and compositing are caught instantly.
4. **Escrow** — 99% of funds are smart-contract-locked. Confirmed fraud → auto-refund.

## FAQ

### Q. Isn't recording videos a hassle?

A. The dedicated camera flow is "start record → unbox → stop record" — about 30 seconds. For 100% fraud protection, this is a fair tradeoff.

### Q. What if I forget to record the unboxing?

A. You forfeit the dispute right. No video = "delivery accepted." Escrow auto-releases.

### Q. What about privacy?

A. Packing/unboxing videos move to a low-cost archive layer after the transaction completes — no longer accessible by third parties. Only hashes remain on-chain as evidence.

### Q. What about logistics-side errors or damage?

A. Tracking data is also recorded on-chain. Logistics issues are arbitrated among seller, buyer, and the carrier. Insurance options will be available.

### Q. What about digital goods?

A. No video needed. Generation ID or encrypted license-key transfer proves delivery. Escrow operates the same way.

### Q. What about high-value items (>$10k)?

A. High-value transactions get additional Validator arbitration, insurance options, and shipping-photo milestones (MORM Phase 1+).
