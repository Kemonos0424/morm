## 4. Features Detail

### 🎬 MORM Cells

3-second WebM segments at 30 fps or below. Reserve the next at 0.3s, purge the old at 1.5s. RAM stays flat.

### 🐝 MORM Swarm

GPU, SSD, bandwidth, uptime — any resource counts. Contributions are auto-rewarded in MORM Token. RTX-6000-class GPUs earn the highest tier; even smartphone bandwidth earns "high" rewards.

### 🔐 Walletless ID

No seed phrase. No private key.
FaceID, TouchID, Windows Hello, console biometrics.
Your usual login becomes a blockchain signature.

### 📦 MORM Shop

P2P commerce. Packing and unboxing videos shot via the dedicated camera embed block hashes as watermarks, making tampering impossible. 99% of payments are escrowed in smart contracts; the operator fee is fixed at 1%.

### 🎨 Generation ID (C2PA-compliant)

Videos created with MORM's built-in generative AI receive a unique ID derived from seed, prompt, and timestamp, anchored on-chain. Your "first one" is protected forever.

C2PA 2.x compliant. Interoperable with Adobe, Google SynthID, OpenAI Sora, and similar ecosystems.

### 🛡️ V-Hash Protection

Perceptual hashing and audio fingerprinting reject copies and clips at upload. Duplicates are garbage-collected network-wide. Only originals remain.

## 8. FAQ

### Q. Do I need to create a wallet?
A. No. Just your phone or PC's biometric authentication. No seed phrases, no private keys to remember.

### Q. How do I post videos?
A. By running a node and holding some MORM Token, you reach the "Creator" level and can post up to 3 videos per day. Even without holding tokens, you can earn posting slots through contribution tasks (AI annotation, etc.).

### Q. Why can the project sustain itself with only a 1% fee?
A. As the network grows, that 1% scales proportionally. The operator team also runs NodePower itself and earns network rewards. More importantly, refusing to concentrate profits is what builds long-term user trust.

### Q. How is illegal content and fraud prevented?
A. Three-layer defense: AI pre-screening, community reports, and Validator review. Violators are subject to Node-Lock, with stake and tokens slashed and the device blacklisted at the biometric-identity level.

### Q. Can I use existing Web3 wallets like MetaMask?
A. Not at launch. MORM prioritizes hiding Web3 complexity entirely and uses its own biometric-based ID system. A future bridge is under consideration.

### Q. Do I need a high-end GPU to run a node?
A. No. Phones and laptops also become part of the network as Light Clients and earn rewards from spare bandwidth while watching. GPUs earn the highest tier, but the door is open to everyone.

### Q. Will my data really never disappear?
A. Transaction evidence and similar critical data are moved to a low-cost archive layer after completion but cannot be tampered with. On the other hand, unwatched videos and consumed cache are auto-cleaned to keep storage efficient.

### Q. Which country is the company in?
A. MORM is not centrally operated by a single legal entity — it's a decentralized network. A core dev team exists, but governance ultimately migrates to a DAO.

### Q. How is MORM different from existing decentralized video SNS like Chingari?
A. We don't claim to be "the first inventor" — we aim to be **the first integrator**. Chingari is a short-video SNS; Livepeer is transcoding; Theta is CDN; C2PA is content provenance; Coinbase Smart Wallet is Walletless ID — all of these already exist as separate projects. MORM unifies them into a single stack, and adds two genuine inventions: **Proof of Physical Evidence (mandatory video-evidence escrow)** and **Slash + biometric permanent ban**.

### Q. Is it compatible with C2PA / Adobe Content Authenticity?
A. Yes. Generation ID is designed C2PA 2.x compliant — interoperable with Adobe, Google SynthID, OpenAI Sora, Numbers Protocol, and similar. It also addresses EU AI Act transparency obligations.

### Q. Is the biometric ban mechanism legally sound?
A. It is designed with GDPR, APPI, CCPA, and similar privacy laws in mind: biometric templates are stored only as hashes, an explicit consent flow is required, and sanctions are tiered. Legal review by qualified counsel in each jurisdiction is required before production launch.

### Q. Is it true that MORM has no company or foundation?
A. Yes. MORM has no legal entity (no Corporation, Foundation, or DAO LLC). All operations run autonomously via Smart Contracts, AI Agents, and the DAO. There is no CEO, no headquarters, no employment contracts. Core contributors can participate pseudonymously or anonymously and receive compensation via Smart Contract Bounty. See AGENTS.md for details.

### Q. Without a legal entity, where does responsibility lie?
A. Traditional "management responsibility" does not exist. Instead: Smart Contract code bugs are addressed by code contributors + Bug Bounty; AI Agent judgement errors are repaired via DAO; illicit content is handled by Moderation Agent + Slash Engine; user-vs-user disputes go through DAO arbitration; regulatory compliance is each user's own responsibility. All decisions are public via the transparency dashboard.
